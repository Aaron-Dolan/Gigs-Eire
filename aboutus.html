About Gigs Eire

Gigs Eire is an innovative platform that connects venues and musicians, revolutionizing the live music scene in Ireland. Founded in 2023 by Aaron Dolan and Brian Candon, Gigs Eire has a deep-rooted passion for music and a strong commitment to supporting the vibrant music community in Ireland.

Aaron and Brian, both talented musicians themselves, recognized the need for a platform that simplifies the process of booking live music events while fostering a thriving music culture. Their shared love for music and their experiences as musicians guided the development of Gigs Eire.

Aaron, originally from Cork, Ireland, is currently living in Konstanz, Germany, where he is pursuing his studies in web development. His technical expertise and musical background bring a unique blend of skills to the Gigs Eire team. Brian, a native of Sligo, Ireland, is deeply rooted in the local music scene and brings his firsthand knowledge of the challenges faced by both musicians and venues.

With Gigs Eire, venues can easily discover talented bands and musicians, making it effortless to book live music events. We offer a comprehensive website and app that provide a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features to facilitate seamless connections between venues and artists.

Our platform empowers musicians by giving them a dedicated space to showcase their skills, expand their fan base, and connect with venues across Ireland. We believe in fostering a thriving live music culture and providing opportunities for both established and emerging artists to shine.

At Gigs Eire, we understand the importance of live music in bringing people together, creating memorable experiences, and contributing to the cultural fabric of Ireland. We are passionate about supporting local talent, promoting diverse music genres, and ensuring that every live music event is a resounding success.

Join us on this exciting journey as we make live music more accessible, vibrant, and enjoyable for venues, musicians, and music lovers across Ireland.
